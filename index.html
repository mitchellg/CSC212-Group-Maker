Group Maker

Where we take the hustle and stress out of group making for school projects! Prepare to have your mind blown away!

A lil' Background

Forming groups for class projects can be a difficult, stressful process. Students often choose the convenience of working with their friends over efficiency. Conversely, students may not have friends to work with and struggle to find a group at all. As a result, groups can become unbalanced where skill sets are not evenly distributed. Furthermore, choosing groups in person is often a hectic process.

Ideally, groups will be evenly distributed among skillsets that are required for the project. We think a website that quickly automates this process by allowing students to enter information about their background and skills, and then algorithmically create groups could solve this problem.

Random group generators do already exist. However, our product differentiates itself by algorithmically pairing people to create optimal groups.
